About Us

We are young game studio based in Kotka, Finland. Our company was founded in 2022 and our first game launched in summer 2022. Our goal and dream is to make fun and unique games that people can enjoy.

Our Team

Our team consists of programmers, modellers, graphic designers and audio engineers. All of us were part of founding this company. We are still game developer students in XAMK.
