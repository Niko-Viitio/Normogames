About Us

We are brand new game studio based in Kotka, Finland. Our company was founded in 2022 and we have already released two games on Steam. Normogames' key to success is its awesome team that can produce everything in house.

The Riflemen

Low poly multiplayer FPS set in WW2. Use bolt-action rifles & knives to defeat your opponents. 7 weapons, 9 maps & 2 gamemodes.

Heal & Hurt

Fight with angles and skeletons in this fast-paced low poly multiplayer arena FPS. 6 weapons, 6 maps & 2 gamemodes.

Platformity

Puzzle platformer with grayscale graphics. Use blocks to progress through the levels and avoid obstacles such as spikes!
