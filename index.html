About Us

We are brand new game studio based in Kotka, Finland. Our company was founded in 2022 and we are releasing our first game same year with many more on the way. Normogames' key to success is our awesome team that can produce everything in house.

Our Team

Our team has 5 people, that includes programmers, modellers, graphic designer and music composer. All of us are founders of Normogames, and all of us are studying game programming at XAMK Kotka.
